## Overview

Darius wins extended trades and punishes greedy Q1 uses. Riven wants short, weaving trades that reset Darius's passive stacks before they become lethal.

## Laning Phase

Stay outside pull range until you have W up. Trade with full Q animation cancels, then disengage with E before Darius can pull or land axe hits.
